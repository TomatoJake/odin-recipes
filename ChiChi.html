Ingredients

  • Ramen Noodles
  • Canned Meat (tuna, chicken, beef jerky, ect.)
  • Dehydrated Vegetables
  • Spices (salt, pepper, garlic powder, hot sauce)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Open the ramen noodle packaging and break the noodles into smaller pieces.
  2. Crush any seasoning packets that come with the noodles.
  3. Open the canned meat and drain any excess liquid.
  4. If using dehydrated vegetables, soak them in a small amount of hot water to rehydrate them.
  5. Boil water in a pot or kettle, using the available means for heating water.
  6. Add the broken ramen noodles to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions.
  7. Once the noodles are almost tender, add the canned meat and dehydrated vegetables.
  8. Stir in the crushed ramen seasoning packets and any additional spices desired.
  9. Let the mixture simmer for a few more minutes until all the ingredients are well-cooked and flavorful.
